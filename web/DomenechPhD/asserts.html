Other Experiments with CFR

We used the invariants generator of iRankFinder to prove the assertions in 13 programs from Sharma et al. [2011]. Without CFR, it proved the assertions for 5 of them, and with CFR it did so for all benchmarks. Also here, Propsdh and Propsc provided the most precise results. The tool of Sharma et al. [2011] is not available so we cannot experimentally compare to them on other benchmarks.

We also achieve similar refinements for the examples of Gulwani et al. [2009].

To check the results we have translated the c source onto an TS. And we have added the assertions on the TSs so that we can compare the computed invariant with the assertion. When executing one example the first result will be the list of nodes of the original TSs where the assertions hold or not and then a second list with the nodes of the refined graph.
